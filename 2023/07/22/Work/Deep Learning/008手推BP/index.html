最后一层神经网络产生的误差

δjL=∂C∂zjL=∂C∂ajL⋅∂ajL∂zjL=∂C∂ajL⋅σ′(zjL)δL=∂C∂aL⊙∂aL∂zL=∇aC⊙σ′(zL)\begin{aligned} & \delta_j^L=\frac{\partial C}{\partial z_j^L}=\frac{\partial C}{\partial a_j^L} \cdot \frac{\partial a_j^L}{\partial z_j^L}=\frac{\partial C}{\partial a_j^L} \cdot \sigma^{\prime}\left(z_j^L\right) \\ & \delta^L=\frac{\partial C}{\partial a^L} \odot \frac{\partial a^L}{\partial z^L}=\nabla_a C \odot \sigma^{\prime}\left(z^L\right) \end{aligned} ​δjL​=∂zjL​∂C​=∂ajL​∂C​⋅∂zjL​∂ajL​​=∂ajL​∂C​⋅σ′(zjL​)δL=∂aL∂C​⊙∂zL∂aL​=∇a​C⊙σ′(zL)​

因此

δL=∇aC⊙σ′(zL)\delta^L=\nabla_a C \odot \sigma^{\prime}\left(z^L\right) δL=∇a​C⊙σ′(zL)

其中$ \odot $表示Hadamard乘积，用于矩阵或向量之间点对点的乘法运算

δjl=∂C∂zjl=∑k∂C∂zkl+1⋅∂zkl+1∂ajl⋅∂ajl∂zjl=∑kδkl+1⋅∂(wkjl+1ajl+bkl+1)∂ajl⋅σ′(zjl)=∑kδkl+1⋅wkjl+1⋅σ′(zjl)\begin{aligned} \delta_j^l=\frac{\partial C}{\partial z_j^l} & =\sum_k \frac{\partial C}{\partial z_k^{l+1}} \cdot \frac{\partial z_k^{l+1}}{\partial a_j^l} \cdot \frac{\partial a_j^l}{\partial z_j^l} \\ & =\sum_k \delta_k^{l+1} \cdot \frac{\partial\left(w_{k j}^{l+1} a_j^l+b_k^{l+1}\right)}{\partial a_j^l} \cdot \sigma^{\prime}\left(z_j^l\right) \\ & =\sum_k \delta_k^{l+1} \cdot w_{k j}^{l+1} \cdot \sigma^{\prime}\left(z_j^l\right) \end{aligned} δjl​=∂zjl​∂C​​=k∑​∂zkl+1​∂C​⋅∂ajl​∂zkl+1​​⋅∂zjl​∂ajl​​=k∑​δkl+1​⋅∂ajl​∂(wkjl+1​ajl​+bkl+1​)​⋅σ′(zjl​)=k∑​δkl+1​⋅wkjl+1​⋅σ′(zjl​)​

∂C∂wjkl=∂C∂zjl⋅∂zjl∂wjkl=δjl⋅∂(wjklakl−1+bjl)∂wjkl=akl−1δjl\frac{\partial C}{\partial w_{j k}^l}=\frac{\partial C}{\partial z_j^l} \cdot \frac{\partial z_j^l}{\partial w_{j k}^l}=\delta_j^l \cdot \frac{\partial\left(w_{j k}^l a_k^{l-1}+b_j^l\right)}{\partial w_{j k}^l}=a_k^{l-1} \delta_j^l ∂wjkl​∂C​=∂zjl​∂C​⋅∂wjkl​∂zjl​​=δjl​⋅∂wjkl​∂(wjkl​akl−1​+bjl​)​=akl−1​δjl​
